 Got here around 9 am today so I could read a book called Establishing Righteous Altars, it was a very good way to begin another learning day!! Using chatgpt I prompted it to give me a pdf that
 dives more into the steps of understanding our ROS2 Window 250 S robot arm. It first explained some of the stuff I learned yesterday such as Trasformation Matrix which is a math conversion system for coordinates. It then focused on understanding the kinematics of our robot. Kinematics is the study of motion taht calucalates postion,velocity and acceleration of each component of our robot. These kinematic break down into two categories: Forward Kinematics and Inverse Kinematics. Forward Kinematics is about calculating the orientation and position of the end-effector which is the part
 of the robot that will pick up the lego pieces. While Inverse Kinematics is about calcualting the postions and orientations of the joints so then you can figure out where you can connect the end-effector.
