Kinematics is the study of motion that calucalates postion,velocity and acceleration without foucisng on what causes that motion like force. For robotics kinematic breaks down into two categories: Forward Kinematics and Inverse Kinematics. Forward Kinematics is about calculating the orientation and position of the end-effector which is the part of the robot that will pick up the lego pieces. While Inverse Kinematics is about calcualting the positions and orientations of the joints so then you can figure out where you can connect the end-effector.
